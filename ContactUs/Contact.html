HELP & SUPPORT FAQS

Looking for assistance? Explore our FAQs to get quick solutions and learn more about what we offer.

How long do your candles burn?

Our candles have an average burn of 40-60 hours, depending on the size. To maximize burn time, allow the wax to melt evenly across the top during each use.

Are your candles made with natural ingredients?

Yes, we use 100% natural soy wax and essential oils for a clean, eco-friendly burn with no harmful chemicals.

Do you offer custom candle scents?

Absolutely! You can create your own custom scent through our website. Simply visit the “candle customization” section to get started.

What's the best way to care for my candle?

Trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each use, burn your candle for at least 2 hours on the first use, and avoid burning it for more than 4 hours at a time for optimal results.
