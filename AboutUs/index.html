ABOUT US
Serenity began in Lebanon when the popular Bath & Body Works candles vanished, leaving a gap in the scented candle market. Initially a personal project, it grew into a full-fledged brand dedicated to bringing warmth and comfort to every space. Each handmade candle offers high-quality, unique scents designed to evoke a sense of calm.
THE OWNER
Hanan Yassine, a law student with a deep passion for candles, turned her hobby into a growing business. She has steadily expanded her brand, combining creativity and entrepreneurship. With each step, she continues to refine her craft and build a loyal customer base.
OUR MISSION
At Serenity, we believe fragrance creates peaceful spaces where relaxation flows naturally.
At Serenity, our mission is to craft candles that transform spaces into sanctuaries. We believe that a candle should not only smell wonderful but should also be made with care, using natural ingredients that allow everyone, even those with sensitivities, to enjoy their soothing aromas. Unlike decorative candles, Serenity candles are designed to be practical and usable, filling homes with fragrance rather than just filling shelves.
WHAT SETS US APART
Serenity distinguishes itself in the Lebanese market with 100% natural, allergen-friendly candles that offer a subtle, enjoyable fragrance.
